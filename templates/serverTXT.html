Server:

  • Total CO2 eq emitted (Kg): The estimated CO2 eq emitted by the server's electricity usage over its entire time of operation starting 16th August 2024, based on the electricity mix in this building. This building is served by an ecological electricity mix (Ökostrom) from water, solar, and wind power [2]. Even though these renewable sources emit no CO2 in the direct conversion of natural energy to electricity, they incur CO2 and other greenhouse gas emissions in their infrastructure, maintenance, and transport [3]. The electricity used by the server is only a fraction of the energy and resources mobilized by a website.
  • Current CO2 eq emissions (Kg): Real-time CO2-equivalent emissions corresponding to current electrical usage assessed directly by the meter on the server.

    The CO2-equivalent mass considers CO2 as well as other greenhouse gas emissions (N2O and CH4) from the entire chain of production of the electricity.
  • Total electricity use (kWh): Total energy use of the server since activation on 16 August 2024. The current and total CO2 equivalent emissions are estimated based on the average emissions from the production of electricity for the EWS Ökostrom mix, which is used in this building [3].
  • Current electrical usage (kWh): Real-time electricity use of the server measured directly by the meter on the server.
